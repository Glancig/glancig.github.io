Glancig

Glancing Classic Games – a free collection of timeless games with a focus on cross‑platform play, privacy, and resistance to censorship.

Features

Classic games: Snake, Tetris, Arkanoid, Minesweeper, Patience, Chess, Checkers, Blackjack.
Cross‑platform: Linux, Windows, macOS, Android, and web (WebAssembly).
Multiplayer: hotseat, LAN, P2P with custom servers (STUN/TURN) to bypass blocks of popular cloud servers.
Privacy: no ads, no tracking, fully open source (GPLv3).
Messenger integration: XMPP / libpurple plugins to invite friends.
